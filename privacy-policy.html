MIRA App – Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use MIRA and explains your privacy rights.

MIRA is designed with privacy in mind. Any content you create—such as video journal entries, mood logs, and reflection prompts—is stored locally on your device by default and is never shared with any third party service by MIRA without your explicit action.

Summary

Your personal reflections and mood data are private. We do not sell your data. We do not monetize through ads. The app is funded through optional paid subscriptions that unlock additional features, so you are never the product. Your data remains yours and stays on your device unless you choose to share or back it up using a service you select.

Collection of Personal Information

MIRA may allow you to optionally provide personal details to enhance your experience, such as:

  • Your name or display name
  • Your preferred language or theme settings
  • Your mood or emotional state for each entry
  • Tags or categories you assign to entries

This information is stored solely within the app to provide its core functionality. It is not transmitted to MIRA servers or any external party unless you choose to use a sync or backup feature.

Use of Personal Information

The personal information you provide is used exclusively to deliver and improve MIRA's features, such as:

  • Saving and displaying your journal entries
  • Tracking mood patterns and entry frequency
  • Providing tailored prompts or insights

Analytics

MIRA may use privacy-friendly, minimal analytics to understand general app usage patterns—such as which screens are most visited—without collecting personally identifiable information or the contents of your entries. No sensitive data is sent to analytics services.

Security of Your Personal Data

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Websites

MIRA may include links to third-party sites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as MIRA evolves. The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent changes. Significant updates will be posted within the app and on our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

  • Through the support section in the app's Settings
