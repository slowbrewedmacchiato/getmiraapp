Features

Video Journaling

Record daily video logs using your front camera. Start with quick 30-second reflections, or unlock longer sessions with MIRA Plus. Each entry becomes a mirror into your mood, mindset, and daily thoughts.

AI Transcription

On‑demand transcription turns your spoken reflections into editable notes. Add context, insights, or gratitude to each entry.

Mood Tracking & State of Mind

Track your mood with emoji‑based scales and see your emotional patterns over time. This powers the State of Mind insights that help you understand your emotional journey.

Reflection Prompts

Built‑in meaningful prompts inspire deeper self‑reflection. Find inspiration when you need it most.

Insights Dashboard

State of Mind view shows mood patterns. Activity Insights reveal your journaling rhythm and peak hours.

Gentle Milestones

Celebrate consistency with meaningful milestones. Share styled achievement cards. No pressured streaks—just honest growth.
